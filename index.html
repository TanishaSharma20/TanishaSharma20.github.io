About The Event

The Naavik National Maritime Olympiad is a prestigious competition designed to identify and nurture talented individuals passionate about maritime industries and ocean sciences.

This national-level event brings together students from across the country to test their knowledge, problem-solving skills, and understanding of maritime concepts. Participants will have the opportunity to showcase their talents on a national platform.

Who Should Participate:

Students from grades 9-12, maritime aspirants, and anyone with an interest in oceanography, naval architecture, shipping management, or marine engineering.

Naavik is a leading EdTech platform specializing in maritime education, with a track record of mentoring over 10,000 students, publishing authoritative books on maritime subjects, and developing innovative solutions like the Smart Helmet for sailor safety.
